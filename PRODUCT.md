# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Primary users are recruiters and hiring managers evaluating Daniil Kulakov for IT support, desktop support, helpdesk, and junior systems operations roles. They need to understand his practical capabilities, evidence of hands-on work, availability, and contact details quickly.

## Product Purpose

This portfolio presents Daniil's support and systems experience in a credible, easy-to-scan format. Success means a hiring manager can assess role fit, review representative projects, open the resume, and start a conversation without friction.

## Brand Personality

Calm, capable, and straightforward. The site should feel technically confident and professional without looking corporate, overly futuristic, or decorative for its own sake.

## Anti-references

- Flashy crypto or fintech interfaces that obscure practical information.
- Generic startup landing pages with vague claims and oversized marketing copy.
- Overly dense CV pages that make key qualifications difficult to scan.

## Design Principles

1. Put evidence before claims: show work, tools, and outcomes clearly.
2. Help recruiters scan: make role fit, experience, availability, and contact routes immediately legible.
3. Keep the technical character calm: use visual depth to support hierarchy, never to distract from content.
4. Make every interaction dependable on mobile, keyboard, and touch devices.
5. Treat language selection as a first-class part of the experience.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA where practical. Preserve strong text contrast, visible keyboard focus, semantic landmarks, accessible dialogs, reduced-motion support, and mobile-friendly controls. Keep the established automatic language selection with a clear manual override.
